## Assignment Objective

Before drawing building layouts, students must analyse the site and propose a first high-level zoning concept.

The goal is to think like a draughting and planning team: understand the land, identify functional zones, plan movement, separate incompatible activities, and list questions before design decisions are made.

## Required Outputs

Each student or assigned group must produce:

1. Site location summary
2. Site analysis notes
3. Public/private/restricted zoning notes
4. Vehicle and pedestrian movement notes
5. Parking and delivery access notes
6. Noisy vs quiet zone notes
7. Security-sensitive zone notes
8. Questions for Ben
9. First zoning concept or diagram
10. First concept board export

## Suggested Precincts

- Public commercial precinct: restaurants, offices, doctors' rooms
- Automotive/workshop precinct: workshops, customer drop-off, service access
- High-security industrial precinct: diamond cutting, gold refinery, jewellers hub
- Auction house precinct: event access, viewing, controlled areas
- Logistics/service precinct: deliveries, waste, maintenance
- Parking and circulation precincts
- Buffer/green/open areas
