# Zoning and Precinct Notes

## Purpose

The Synergy Inc. project should first be planned at precinct level. A 24-hectare site is large enough to divide into functional zones before detailed building layouts are drawn.

## Suggested Precincts

### 1. Public Commercial Precinct

Possible functions:

- Restaurants
- Offices
- Doctors' rooms
- Public reception areas
- Visitor parking

Planning notes:

- Should be easy for the public to access.
- Should feel safe and professional.
- Should be separated from noisy workshop activity.
- Should have clear parking and pedestrian routes.

### 2. Automotive and Workshop Precinct

Possible functions:

- Mechanical workshops
- Vehicle service bays
- Customer drop-off
- Parts receiving
- Workshop admin
- Workshop parking and yard space

Planning notes:

- Needs strong vehicle flow planning.
- Should consider noise and fumes.
- Should allow parts delivery access.
- Should not conflict with restaurant or medical users.

### 3. High-Security Industrial Precinct

Possible functions:

- Diamond cutting house
- Gold refinery
- Jewellers hub
- Secure storage
- Controlled delivery/collection

Planning notes:

- Must be separated from general public movement.
- Requires access control.
- May require perimeter security.
- Should include secure service routes.
- Should be reviewed by Cyber Security and operational/security mentors.

### 4. Auction House Precinct

Possible functions:

- Auction hall
- Viewing area
- Registration or reception
- Storage or staging area
- Visitor parking
- Security checkpoint

Planning notes:

- Could have high visitor traffic during events.
- Needs clear entry and exit flow.
- May need controlled access between public and restricted areas.

### 5. Logistics and Service Precinct

Possible functions:

- Delivery area
- Waste handling
- Maintenance access
- Plant/service equipment
- Loading areas

Planning notes:

- Should be functional and not in the main public-facing view.
- Should connect logically to workshops, restaurants, auction house, and industrial areas.

## Student Deliverable

Create a first zoning concept showing where each precinct could be located and explain the reasoning behind the layout.
