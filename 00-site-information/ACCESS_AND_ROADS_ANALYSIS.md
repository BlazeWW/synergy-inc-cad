# Access and Roads Analysis

## Purpose

The purpose of this file is to analyse how people, vehicles, service vehicles, and secure deliveries may enter, move through, and leave the Synergy Inc. site.

## Access Types to Consider

### Public Access

Used by:

- Restaurant visitors
- Office visitors
- Doctors' room patients
- Auction house visitors
- Workshop customers

### Staff Access

Used by:

- Office staff
- Restaurant staff
- Medical staff
- Workshop staff
- Auction house staff
- Jewellery, diamond, and refinery staff

### Service and Delivery Access

Used by:

- Food and restaurant deliveries
- Workshop parts deliveries
- Waste collection
- Maintenance vehicles
- General suppliers

### High-Security Access

Used by:

- Diamond cutting operations
- Gold refinery operations
- Jewellers hub operations
- Security personnel
- Controlled deliveries and collections

## Student Tasks

- Identify possible entrances and exits.
- Separate public traffic from secure industrial traffic where possible.
- Consider whether the site needs more than one gate.
- Consider whether the auction house needs separate event traffic flow.
- Consider workshop customer drop-off and collection areas.
- Consider emergency access.

## Questions to Resolve

- Where should the main public entrance be?
- Should heavy vehicles use a separate entrance?
- Should high-security deliveries use a separate controlled access route?
- Does the site need a gatehouse?
- Should doctors' rooms and restaurants share parking?
- Should mechanical workshops have separate service yards?
