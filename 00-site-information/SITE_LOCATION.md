## Site Size

The site is approximately 24 hectares.

## Scale Note

24 hectares is approximately 240,000 square metres.

This is a large site. Students should think at precinct level first before drawing individual buildings.

## Initial Site Thinking

The first draughting task is not to design final buildings. The first task is to understand the site and propose how the different functions may be grouped.

Primary precincts to consider:

- Public commercial area: restaurants, offices, doctors' rooms
- Automotive area: mechanical workshops, customer access, workshop service access
- High-security industrial area: diamond cutting house, gold refinery, jewellers hub
- Auction house area: visitor access, controlled movement, event flow
- Logistics and delivery area
- Staff and operational access
- Parking zones
- Landscaped or buffer zones
- Security-controlled zones

## Information Still Required

Before detailed design work begins, the following information should be confirmed:

- Exact site boundaries
- Existing access roads
- Existing structures, if any
- Municipal zoning restrictions
- Services availability: water, electricity, sewage, stormwater, fibre
- Preferred entrance and exit points
- Development phasing plan
- Security requirements for diamond, gold, and jewellery operations
- Heavy-vehicle access requirements
- Parking expectations
- Any environmental or neighbouring-property constraints
