# Questions for Ben

Use this file to collect questions before moving from site analysis into detailed layouts.

## Site and Boundary Questions

- Are there formal site boundary drawings available?
- Are there any existing buildings on the property?
- Are there existing roads or entrances?
- Is there a preferred main entrance?
- Are there existing services on the site?

## Municipal and Planning Questions

- What is the current zoning of the land?
- Are there restrictions on commercial, industrial, medical, or refinery-related use?
- Is the development intended to happen in phases?
- Are any environmental studies required?

## Business Priority Questions

- Which facilities must be planned first?
- Which facilities are optional or future-phase items?
- Should the mechanical workshops be a major public-facing anchor?
- Should the restaurants and doctors' rooms form a public-facing commercial area?
- Should the auction house be close to public access or within a controlled zone?

## Security Questions

- What level of access control is expected for the diamond cutting house?
- What level of access control is expected for the gold refinery?
- Should the jewellers hub be public-facing, restricted, or semi-restricted?
- Will there be secure deliveries or collections?
- Should there be a gatehouse or controlled security checkpoint?

## Operations Questions

- Will heavy vehicles need access?
- Will customers bring vehicles directly to workshops?
- Will the workshops need external yard space?
- How much parking should be planned initially?
- Should staff parking be separated from visitor parking?
