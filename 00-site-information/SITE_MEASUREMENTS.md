# Site Measurements

## Known Measurement

The site is approximately 24 hectares.

24 hectares = approximately 240,000 square metres.

## Measurement Tasks for Students

Students should use the map reference and available tools to estimate or document:

- Approximate site shape
- Approximate length and width, if visible
- Possible road frontage
- Possible access points
- Rough distance between major proposed zones
- Area required for major precincts

## Important Measurement Rule

Any measurements taken from Google Maps, screenshots, or visual estimation must be labelled as approximate.

Do not present approximate measurements as final survey information.

## Required Label Format

Use labels such as:

- Approximate
- Estimated from Google Maps
- To be confirmed by survey
- Assumption pending confirmation
