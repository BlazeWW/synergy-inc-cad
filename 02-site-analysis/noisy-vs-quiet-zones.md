# Noisy vs Quiet Zones

## Objective

Separate uses that may conflict because of noise, fumes, traffic, or privacy.

## Noisy / Active Uses

- Mechanical workshops
- Service yards
- Delivery areas
- Auction event traffic
- Industrial operations

## Quiet / Sensitive Uses

- Doctors' rooms
- Office spaces
- Restaurants
- Public waiting areas

## Student Tasks

- Identify which uses should be buffered from each other.
- Suggest where green/open buffer areas could help.
- Suggest where service routes should be hidden or separated.
- Explain how the zoning concept protects quieter spaces.
