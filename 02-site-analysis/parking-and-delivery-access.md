# Parking and Delivery Access

## Objective

Identify practical parking and delivery requirements for the different precincts.

## Parking Types

- Visitor parking
- Staff parking
- Doctors' room patient parking
- Restaurant parking
- Workshop customer parking
- Auction event parking
- Secure industrial staff parking

## Delivery Access Types

- Restaurant deliveries
- Workshop parts deliveries
- Office deliveries
- Auction house logistics
- High-security deliveries
- Waste collection

## Student Tasks

- Suggest which parking areas can be shared.
- Suggest which parking areas should be separated.
- Identify delivery routes that should not pass through public pedestrian areas.
- Consider heavy-vehicle movement.
