# Vehicle and Pedestrian Flow

## Objective

Plan safe, logical movement for vehicles and pedestrians.

## Vehicle Groups

- Customer vehicles
- Staff vehicles
- Workshop vehicles
- Delivery vehicles
- Heavy vehicles
- Emergency vehicles
- Secure transport vehicles

## Pedestrian Groups

- Restaurant visitors
- Office visitors
- Doctors' room patients
- Auction visitors
- Workshop customers
- Staff
- Security personnel

## Student Tasks

- Identify routes that should not cross.
- Identify where pedestrian paths are needed.
- Identify possible customer drop-off areas.
- Identify where service vehicles should move.
- Identify security-controlled movement areas.
