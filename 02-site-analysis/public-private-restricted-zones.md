# Public, Private, and Restricted Zones

## Objective

Separate the Synergy Inc. site into public, private, operational, and restricted zones.

## Public Areas

Examples:

- Restaurants
- Office reception
- Doctors' rooms
- Auction registration or public viewing
- Customer parking

## Private / Staff Areas

Examples:

- Staff offices
- Workshop staff areas
- Back-of-house restaurant areas
- Medical staff areas

## Operational Areas

Examples:

- Mechanical workshops
- Parts deliveries
- Loading/service yards
- Maintenance areas

## Restricted / High-Security Areas

Examples:

- Diamond cutting house
- Gold refinery
- Secure jewellery handling
- Secure storage
- Controlled delivery/collection areas

## Student Deliverable

Prepare a basic zoning diagram showing these areas and explain why they were separated.
