## Task 4: Synergy Inc. Physical Facility Security Design Notes

This section translates digital defense concepts into physical security designs across high-value architectural zones based on standard defense-in-depth security principles.

### 1. High-Value Core Assets
*   **Diamond Cutting House:** 
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Targeted asset theft, employee collusion, and physical security bypass attempts.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Implement dual-custody access patterns requiring two separate credentials to release entry locks. Construct boundaries out of high-strength reinforced concrete integrated with structural seismic vibration sensors. Ensure continuous CCTV coverage utilizing high-frame-rate cameras angled directly at raw gemstone handling benches.
*   **Gold Refinery:**
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Commodity diversion, dangerous high-heat process tampering, and hazardous chemical spills.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Install secure two-door mantrap airlocks at all entry and exit paths to prevent rapid escape during an incident. Require automated, immutable logging of air quality and chemical storage systems. Restrict keycard authorization to personnel with up-to-date hazardous safety compliance records.
*   **Jewellers Hub:**
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Quick smash-and-grab attacks, arm-twisting robberies, and retail counter shrinkage.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Display cases must be constructed from smash-resistant polycarbonate materials. Inventory must be transferred nightly into time-locked biometric safes. Install discrete foot-operated silent panic buttons behind workstation panels, connected directly to local security forces.

### 2. Support Operations & Access Interlocks
*   **Auction House Operations:**
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Public crowd control issues, opportunistic theft of high-profile items on display, and asset tracking failures.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Establish separate public and private zones. High-value lots must be staged inside temporary secure storage cages behind the podium. Implement mandatory electronic guest badge printing and identity verification at the primary reception counters.
*   **Staff-Only Administrative Zones:**
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Unregistered visitor tailgating, corporate espionage, and hardware theft.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Secure all perimeter barriers with RFID access controls. Enforce technical anti-passback controls to ensure an access badge cannot be passed backward to enter a door twice without first exiting.
*   **Delivery & Restricted Access Infrastructure:**
    *   *Threat Analysis:* Forced vehicle entry and transport security failures.
    *   *Controls Matrix:* Route all commercial transits through interlocking vehicle sally ports (double-gated staging spaces where delivery trucks are locked in during inspection). Enforce mandatory digital logging of government-issued driver credentials, and install crash-rated physical bollards at entry thresholds.

## 2. Physical RBAC Matrix
This checklist translates digital roles into physical keycard and biometric access permissions for the building layout.

*   **Public Visitors / Auction Attendees:**
    *   *Access Allowed:* Public Lobbies, Auction House seating floors, public restrooms.
    *   *Access Denied:* Diamond cutting rooms, Gold refinery, Jewellers workshop, staff back offices.
*   **General Staff / Admin Personnel:**
    *   *Access Allowed:* Public zones, staff break rooms, corporate offices, general inventory delivery docks.
    *   *Access Denied:* High-value secure zones (Refinery, Diamond Vaults, Server Rooms).
*   **Specialized Technicians (Jewellers & Refiners):**
    *   *Access Allowed:* Specific workshops matching their clearance (e.g., Diamond Cutting House or Gold Refinery), staff areas.
    *   *Access Denied:* Auction floor administrative cash vaults or master network server racks.
*   **Security & Facilities Operations:**
    *   *Access Allowed:* Universal physical access across all zones (including Restricted Points, Server Rooms, and Vaults).
    *   *Security Enforcement:* Entry into highest-value zones must require **Dual-Authorization** (two security personnel scanning together) and automatically trigger time-stamped log entries.
